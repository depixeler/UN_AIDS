Innovation as a Service

We involve you at every step of our Innovation-as-a-Service journey


As the world continues to evolve in the post-COVID era, the rate at which technologies become obsolete remains unaffected. If anything, ‘change is the only constant’ is more significant than ever in a world that is embracing the new normal by recruiting new technologies to stay relevant. What’s new today becomes a thing of the past tomorrow.

UNAIDS Digital Garage’s Innovation-as-a-Service platform helps our customers keep up with changing technologies, to ensure that they stay ahead of the competitive curve and be first movers in their respective spheres.

From involving our customers in populating their idea funnels, to filtering out unscalable ideas by focusing on their core objective, to quickly rolling out their Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) through rapid prototyping, we involve our customers at every step of our Innovation-as-a-Service journey.
